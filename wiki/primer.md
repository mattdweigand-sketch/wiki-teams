# Wiki Primer

By-question-type routing into the wiki. Customize the sections below to fit your domain — these are starter prompts.

Use this when a question is fuzzy and you're not sure which entity type to start with.

---

## "What is X?"

Definition or canonical explanation of a term, product, or concept.

- For domain terms → start at [`glossary.md`](glossary.md)
- For company-specific terms → check [`concepts/`](concepts/)
- For products → [`products/`](products/)

## "Who uses X?" or "Who's the buyer?"

Persona and customer questions.

- Persona profile → [`personas/`](personas/)
- Specific named customer → [`customers/`](customers/)

## "How does X work?"

Feature mechanics, workflows, internal logic.

- Feature page → [`features/`](features/)
- Underlying concept → [`concepts/`](concepts/)

## "How do we compete with X?" or "What's our positioning?"

Competitive and strategy questions.

- Competitor profile → [`competitors/`](competitors/)
- Strategic decision → [`decisions/`](decisions/)
- Active strategic bet → [`initiatives/`](initiatives/)

## "What's our KPI on X?" or "How do we measure Y?"

- Metric definitions → [`metrics/`](metrics/)

## "Why did we choose X?" or "What did we decide about Y?"

- Decision record → [`decisions/`](decisions/)

## "Who's responsible for X?" or "What does the X team do?"

- People and teams → [`people/`](people/)

## "Have we analyzed X before?"

- Existing analyses → [`analyses/`](analyses/) (auto-filed cross-page syntheses)

---

If the question doesn't map cleanly here, scan [`index.md`](index.md) — the master catalog.
