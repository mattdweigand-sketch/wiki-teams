# Company Overview

*Write a paragraph or two about your company here. The ingest agent will update this as it processes sources — but a hand-written seed gives downstream agents an anchor.*

Suggested structure:

- **What we do** — one-sentence description of the product or service.
- **Who we serve** — primary customer / persona segments.
- **How we make money** — revenue model in plain language.
- **What we believe** — the strategic thesis or theory of change.
- **Where we are now** — stage, scale, current focus.

Keep it short. Detail belongs in dedicated entity pages, not here.
